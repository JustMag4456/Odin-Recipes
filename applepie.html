The Apple Pie Recipe:

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 6–7 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp are great), peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (50g) brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp butter (to dot on top before baking)

For the Crust:

  • Use store-bought pie dough (2 sheets) or your favorite homemade pie crust

Optional for top:

  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1 tbsp sugar (for sprinkling)

Instruction (Step-by-step)

Preheat and Prepare Crust

  • Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  • Roll out one pie crust and place it into a 9-inch pie dish. Let the edges hang over slightly.

Make the Filling

  • In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples with both sugars, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and lemon juice. Mix well until apples are coated.

Fill the Pie

  • Pour the apple filling into the prepared crust.
  • Dot the top with small pieces of butter.

Top the Pie

  • Place the second crust over the apples.
  • Full top crust (cut slits to vent).
  • Seal and crimp the edges with your fingers or a fork.
  • Brush with a beaten egg and sprinkle a bit of sugar for a golden, crunchy top.

Bake

  • Bake at 220°C (425°F) for 15 minutes.
  • Lower the heat to 180°C (350°F) and bake for another 35–45 minutes until the crust is golden and filling is bubbling.
  • If the edges brown too fast, cover them with foil.

Cool and Serve

  • Let the pie cool for at least 2 hours before slicing. This helps the filling set properly.
