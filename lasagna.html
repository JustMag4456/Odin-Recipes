The Lasagna Recipe:

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or half beef, half sausage)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jar (about 700g / 24 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tsp Italian seasoning or dried oregano

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 450g (15 oz) ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)

Instruction (Step-by-step)

Cook the Meat Sauce

  • In a large skillet, heat a little olive oil.
  • Sauté the chopped onion until soft (about 3–4 minutes).
  • Add garlic, cook for 30 seconds.
  • Add the ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.
  • Stir in the marinara sauce and optional Italian seasoning. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Prepare the Cheese Filling

  • In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

Cook the Noodles (if needed)

  • If using regular noodles, boil according to package directions.
  • Drain and set aside on a flat surface to avoid sticking.

Assemble the Lasagna

In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer the ingredients:

  • Thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  • A layer of noodles.
  • Half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  • A layer of meat sauce.
  • Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  • Repeat the layering (noodles → ricotta → sauce → mozzarella).
  • Finish with a final layer of noodles, remaining sauce, and mozzarella on top.

Bake

  • Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes.
  • Remove foil and bake an extra 10–15 minutes, until the top is bubbly and golden.

Cool and Serve

  • Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before cutting. This helps it firm up.
