The cake recipe:

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or half beef, half sausage)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jar (about 700g / 24 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tsp Italian seasoning or dried oregano

For the cheese Layer:

  • 450g (15 oz) ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Instruction (Step-by-step)

Cook the Meat Sauce:

  • In a large pan, heat a bit of olive oil.
  • Add chopped onion and cook until soft (about 3–4 mins).
  • Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  • Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.
  • Pour in the marinara sauce and optional seasonings. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Prepare the Cheese Mixture

  • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.

Boil the Noodles (if needed)

  • If using regular lasagna noodles, boil them according to the package.
  • Drain and set aside on a flat surface to prevent sticking.

Assemble the Lasagna

In a baking dish (9x13 inch), layer as follows:

  • A thin layer of meat sauce.
  • A layer of noodles.
  • Half of the ricotta mixture.
  • A layer of meat sauce.
  • Sprinkle mozzarella.
  • Repeat steps 2–5 once more.
  • Top with a final layer of noodles, sauce, and mozzarella.

Bake

  • Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes.
  • Remove foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes, until the cheese on top is bubbly and golden.

Rest and Serve

  • Let it sit for 10–15 minutes before cutting. This helps it hold its shape.
